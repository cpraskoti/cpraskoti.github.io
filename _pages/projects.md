## PRISM: Process-Reward-Guided Routing for Visual Reasoning
<!-- **Advisor: [Dr. Weizi Li](https://weizi-li.github.io/), [Fluidic City Lab](https://fluidic-city.github.io/), University of Tennessee** -->

A routing framework that dynamically allocates visual reasoning queries between small and large Vision-Language Models. PRISM uses learned process-reward signals to decide when a smaller model suffices and when to escalate to a larger one, optimizing the accuracy–efficiency tradeoff through adaptive inference.

## Perception-Driven Bimanual Robotic Manipulation
<!-- **Advisor: [Dr. Jindong Tan](https://tickle.utk.edu/bme/faculty/jindong-tan/), University of Tennessee** -->

Research on bimanual robotic grasping and manipulation for delicate medical applications. Combines Vision-Language Models with imitation learning for embodied AI, including dataset development and training/evaluation pipelines for perception-driven policies.


## 2D/3D Co-Simulation Environment
<!-- **Advisor: [Dr. Weizi Li](https://weizi-li.github.io/), [Fluidic City Lab](https://fluidic-city.github.io/), University of Tennessee** -->

A traffic co-simulation framework that generates elevation-aware 3D road networks from real-world GIS data to support large-scale traffic flow simulation and high-fidelity vehicle dynamics evaluation.

## MIAT: Maneuver-Intention-Aware Transformer
<!-- **Advisor: [Dr. Weizi Li](https://weizi-li.github.io/), [Fluidic City Lab](https://fluidic-city.github.io/), University of Tennessee** -->

A mixed-traffic trajectory prediction framework that integrates maneuver-intention awareness with spatiotemporal interaction modeling. Achieved 11.1% improvement in long-horizon predictions by explicitly modeling driver maneuver intentions through spatio-temporal attention. [[Paper]](https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.05059)

## Metahuman Interview Agent

A multimodal interview agent (speech-to-text, text-to-speech, LLM) that conducts STAR-style interviews and produces rubric-based feedback; packaged as a reusable service with evaluation checklists.

## Continual Learning with Hard Attention Parameter Masking

Research on enabling neural networks to learn new image classification tasks without catastrophic forgetting by using hard attention mechanisms to selectively mask and protect important parameters.

## ECC Encrypted Ad-hoc Sensor Network

Weather data collection network deployed over Kathmandu Valley demonstrating the efficiency of Elliptic Curve Cryptography (ECC) over RSA for resource-constrained embedded systems in wireless sensor networks.
