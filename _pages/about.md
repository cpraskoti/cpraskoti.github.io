I'm a graduate research student in Computer Science at the University of Tennessee. I work on multimodal reasoning and adaptive inference in foundation models (LLM, VLM, VLA), with applications in visual reasoning and decision-making.

Before graduate school, I worked as an ML engineer for 4 years building production systems including LLM-based generation workflows, conversational AI agents, and forecasting pipelines. I approach research the same way I approached production systems: reproducible experiments, rigorous evaluation, and methods that hold up beyond the benchmark.

## Technical Skills

**Research Areas:** Multimodal Reasoning, Vision-Language Models, Adaptive Inference, Embodied AI, Spatiotemporal Modeling, Continual Learning

**ML/DL:** PyTorch, TensorFlow, Transformers, LLMs, VLMs, RAG, Embeddings, Ranking/Retrieval, NLP, Computer Vision, Time-Series

**Languages & Core:** Python, C/C++, SQL

**MLOps & Software:** Docker, Kubernetes, CI/CD, MLflow, HuggingFace, REST APIs, Model Serving

**Cloud & Data:** AWS, Azure, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Neo4j, Vector Search
